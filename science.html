Webb uncovers a hidden giant planet orbiting Beta Pictoris

NASA's Webb telescope found Beta Pictoris d, a world at least twice Jupiter's mass hidden inside a bright debris disk 63 light-years away, detected by its atmosphere's chemical fingerprint rather than a direct image.

02

Antarctica offers decades of warning on sea level rise

A Nature study finds Antarctic ice loss stays reliably predictable until about mid-century, giving governments a 30-to-50-year window to plan for the sea level rise it will bring.

03

Fluoride-free gel regrows damaged tooth enamel in the lab

University of Nottingham researchers built a fluoride-free gel that draws calcium and phosphate from saliva to rebuild a mineral layer matching real enamel, with a first product targeted for 2027.

04

Fossils from Japan reveal a new genus of giant salamander

Vertebrae collected in the 1990s turn out to belong to Limnospondylus ajimuensis, a new genus of giant salamander that grew to about a meter some 3.5 million years ago in Kyushu.
